Site Target Audience Color Scheme Typography

Light Blue

#c4f0ff

This light blue is used for the the text in the header, footer, and navigation. It's also the background on the navigation when you hover over it. The background of the whole page uses this same hex code, but at 60% opacity.



Medium Blue

#15109A

The medium blue is primarily used for the backgrounds on the header and the footer.



Dark Blue

#120E76

The dark blue is used to separate the navigation bar from the header. It is also used as the text color when you hover over the navigation.
