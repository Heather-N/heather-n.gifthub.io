Personas

Alice Johnson

Alice is 37, married, and has two kids. She has a bachelor's degree in education and works as a teacher teaching the fourth grade.

Barry Quint

Barry is 52 and married. He has three kids and two grandchildren. He has a bachelor's degree in journalism and is currently working at a local newspaper.

Scenarios

  1. Alice would go to WeatherTru to find out the weather to appropriately dress her kids for the day.
  2. Barry would check WeatherTru in the winter to see if it's clear enough to drive to visit his children.
  3. Alice would go to WeatherTru to discover if the weather will permit her kids to have soccer practice.
  4. Barry would look at WeatherTru to see if they will need to make accomodations in delivering the newspaper everyday.
  5. Alice would need WeatherTru to find out if there would be any scientific events she could take her class on a fieldtrip to see.
