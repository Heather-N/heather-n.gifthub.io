Design Principles



White Space

Pandora fits the design principle of white space, leaving a clean design. Since the main point of the website is to listen to music, it gives you what you need to do so without giving too much. There's no unneeded information or options just to fill up space. The design is clean, making it much easier to understand and navigate.
