Idaho Governor Not so Sure on President Biden’s Vaccine Projections

Eric Grossarth | March 3, 2021 | East Idaho News

BOISE — President Joe Biden promised enough COVID-19 vaccine for every adult by May, but Idaho Gov. Brad Little is not so sure.

“I don’t think the math adds up,” Gov. Little said during an Idaho Press Club legislative headliner on Wednesday.

Biden made his announcement Tuesday, the same day Little and other governors spoke with White House COVID Coordinator Jeff Zients. Little said numbers given during the call do not reach the need to have a vaccine into the arm of every adult Idahoan by May.
